About Me

This is title of the news

Hello! Welcome to Everyday with Magaen J!. My name is Magaen J. Sometimes I may refer to myself as MJ. I created this site for many reasons. First, this blog allows me to share different ways for you to enjoy everyday life.I'm so amazed at how far we have advanced with technology to be able to share things through blogs and other social media platforms. We only have one life and I'm determined to help everyone that I can live life to the fullest. My passion and purpose in life are to help others. Growing up, I was always told, " You have wisdom beyond your years." God has blessed me with a GIFT "to help." Typically when GIFTS are given to you, it's not for you to hang on to it but to share it with others so that they may also be blessed.

I hold many titles, but first and foremost, I am an ambassador for Christ. Second, I am a mother. Next, I am a friend, daughter, sister, cousin, god-mother and the list goes on lol. Finally, by profession, I am an Entrepreneur, Therapist, Coach, Trainer, Mediator, and Consultant. As you can imagine, I have a pretty busy life, but through my journey, I've learned tons of golden nuggets that I plan to share with you. My purpose is to equip, empower, educate and encourage my brothers and sisters. If you have a question or would like more information about a specific topic, please feel free to leave me a comment. I will admit, I'm more of a talker than a writer so many of my posts will include short videos. Please check out and subscribe to my Youtube page Everydaywithmagaenj. I plan to post a new video weekly.I hope that you're blessed with the videos and this blog.

Enjoy!

Magaen J
